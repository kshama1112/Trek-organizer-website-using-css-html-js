Quick Itinerary


Get picked up from your preferred pickup locations between 11:00 PM to 12:30 AM in shared transfers and reach the base camp by 1:30 AM (approx).
You will start the Skandagiri trek late at night around 3:00 AM.
Soak in the serene views as you reach the summit at 6:00 AM and relax for a while.
Wait for the sunrise till 6:15 AM (approx.) and get awestruck with a spectacular sight.
Start descending to the base of Skandagiri at 7:30 AM after the peaceful time spent at the top.
By 12:00 noon, you will be dropped off at your respective locations in Bangalore with a bag filled with cherishable memories.
